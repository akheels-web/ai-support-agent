Title: VPN Not Connecting

Do not suggest random restart as first solution.

Questions:
1. Are you connected to the internet?
2. What VPN error do you see?
3. Is MFA prompt appearing?
4. Are you using company laptop?

Troubleshooting:
1. Confirm internet works.
2. Confirm date and time are correct.
3. Confirm username is correct.
4. If MFA fails, create ticket for identity team.
5. If VPN times out, create ticket for network team.

Escalation:
Group: Service Desk
Priority: Medium
