Title: Password Reset

Important:
- AI must not reset passwords directly.
- AI must verify caller first.
- AI must not reveal sensitive account information.

Questions:
1. Do you remember your current password?
2. Do you have access to MFA?
3. Are you using a company-managed device?

Action:
1. If verified, create password reset ticket.
2. If not verified, create unverified caller ticket.
3. If MFA is unavailable, escalate to Service Desk.

Escalation:
Group: Service Desk
Priority: High
